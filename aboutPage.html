About us

Our team

Andriy Shestopalko

Andriy is both an attorney and an international business consultant. Prior to forming Shestopalko Law, Andriy served as an Attorney Advisor at the United States Department of Justice – Newark Immigration Court.

An immigrant to the United States himself, Andriy has personally experienced the challenges faced by foreign nationals moving to this country. Andriy previously held positions with the United States Department of Justice Office of Immigration Litigation, Supreme Court of Indiana, New York State – Ontario County District Attorney’s Office, a leading international business consultancy firm, and a boutique New York City immigration firm.

Andriy’s immigration practice focuses on investor and employment-based immigration, family immigration and humanitarian immigration; Andriy’s corporate practice focuses on transaction management, cross-border transactions, businesses formation and governance, mergers and acquisitions, and assistance for foreign companies seeking to enter the United States market; and Andriy’s tax law practice focuses on corporate regulatory compliance, and asset and wealth management.
